Eligibility- the Full Refund Option entitles customers to recieve a full refund for their cooking upon cancellation. The fee is charged per passenger, per booking and most be purchaed for all passengers on the reservation at the same time. To be eligible for a refund, the booking most be cancelled a minimum of one(1) day before scheduled departure time fo the first leg of the departing journey.

To request a refund for a booking that you cancelled that is not eligible for an online refund, please complete the online Refund Request Form or contact our Call Centre at 1-888-619-8622.
